Features:

- WASD - movement

- Q / E - left / right

- T / G- place / delete block

- R / F - up / down

- P - print out world state (check console)

- Press down left click to pan camera

- 32x32x32 world of blocks

Notes:

- Trying to add or delete blocks outside of the 32x32x32 area causes errors

- Looking directly up or down causes the camera to spin/bug out

- Cube vertices and UVs are drawn all together per cube

(default performance might seem low but that's because the entire floor is made of individually destructable cubes)
